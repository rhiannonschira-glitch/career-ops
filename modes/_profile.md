**Rhiannon has a stable 16-year healthcare job she can stay at.** She is NOT desperate. She will NOT leave for something mundane, repetitive, underpaid, or traditional nursing.

A job must either be:
1. **Clearly safer/better than her current role**, OR
2. **Meaningfully aligned with healthcare innovation, AI, clinical technology, product building, workflow improvement, or patient/caregiver impact**

## Critical: NOT an Engineer

**Rhiannon is NOT a software engineer and does NOT want engineering roles.**

She uses AI tools (Claude Code, etc.) to build things, but she is not a coder by training. Her value is:
- **Clinical domain expertise** (16 years bedside)
- **Workflow and process design** (operations manuals, workflow standardization)
- **AI tool proficiency** (using AI to build, not writing code from scratch)
- **Product thinking** (understanding what clinicians need)
- **Training and enablement** (teaching others to adopt technology)

**Auto-reject any role with:**
- "Engineer" in the title (Software, ML, AI, Data, Platform, etc.)
- Coding requirements (Python, JavaScript, SQL as primary skill)
- "Build production systems" language
- Engineering team membership

**Target roles that value:**
- Clinical subject matter expertise
- Workflow design and process improvement
- AI adoption and enablement (training others to use AI)
- Product feedback and clinical validation
- Implementation and customer success
- Operations and care coordination
